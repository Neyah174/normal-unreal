# NORMAL
### An interactive liminal space experience built in Unreal Engine 5

> *"Disturb the comfortable. Comfort the disturbed."*

---

## What is this?

NORMAL is a first-person interactive art experience where you explore a house that slowly stops being one.

There are no enemies. No jump scares. No objectives.

The more you notice something is wrong, the more wrong it becomes.

---

## Concept

You wake up in a house. Everything is fine. The lighting is overcast. It's quiet. You can wander.

But the longer you explore — the more you open drawers, look at pictures, stand in certain spots — a hidden **perception counter** increments. And as it rises, the world responds:

- Textures begin to breathe
- Shadows stop matching their light sources  
- Rooms you've already visited are slightly different now
- The hum starts
- The house becomes somewhere else

The experience moves through **3 distortion levels**, from a familiar grey-lit home to something that feels like a place that was always pretending.
